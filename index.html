About

We are the Sopra Steria Million Makers team. We are taking on the Million Makers challenge to raise money for the Princes Trust charity. Find out more below.

Million Makers

Million Makers is an entrepreneurial fundraising challenge in which a different teams compete to raise atleast Â£10,000 in six months. All money raised from this challenge is used to tansform the lives of disadvantaged young people around the country.

Find more here

Princes Trust

Prince's Trust is a United Kingdom based charity founded in 1976 by Charles, Prince of Wales to help young people. Princes trust works with 60,000 young people annually helping around 80% of them move on to Employment, Education, Training or Volunteering.

Sopra Steria

Sopra Steria is a European leader in digital transformation established in September 2014 upon the merger of Sopra and Steria. The company provides one of the most comprehensive portfolios of end-to-end service offerings on the market.

Timeline

The next 6 months.

  • August 2016

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The Sopra Steria team is introduced to the Million Makers challenge an start brainstorming for ideas to raise money.

  • September 2016

    Dragons Den | Community Matters

    The business plan is submitted, the Sopra Steria team head down to Glasgow to secure the seed funding through a dragons den style pitch.
    A Sopra Steria internal charity week is held with many activities such as space hopper trials, bake sales and many more.

  • October - November

    Various Activites

    The Sopra Steria team are busy advertising the cause, selling Burns supper ticket, collecting dontaions from football games and other events and actively selling wristbands.

  • December

    Christmas Raffle

    Christmas raffles takes place. Final preparation is made for burns supper. The Sopra Steria can enjoy some time off for the festive holidays!

  • January

    Burns Supper

    The big event is here! The Burns supper night and auction are held.

  • Febuary

    Counting The Money

    The challenge comes to an end. All closing activities are done and the final profits are caluclated.
